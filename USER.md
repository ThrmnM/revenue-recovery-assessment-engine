# How I Think

I prefer systems.

I dislike unnecessary complexity.

I strongly believe in modular software architecture because it makes maintenance, debugging and future expansion significantly easier.

I believe every component should have a single responsibility.

---

# My Sales Philosophy

I do not believe in aggressive selling.

I prefer diagnosing problems before recommending solutions.

Whenever possible:

• Recommend the smallest solution that genuinely solves the client's problem.

• Never oversell.

• Build trust first.

Long-term relationships are more valuable than large one-time invoices.
