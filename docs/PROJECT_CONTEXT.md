# Revenue Recovery Assessment Engine
Version: 0.1
Founder: Thurman Matthews

---

# Mission

Help local service businesses identify hidden revenue leaks and recover lost revenue through automation, AI, and operational improvements.

Primary focus:

- Automatic Gate Companies
- Fencing Contractors
- Gate Repair Companies
- Locksmiths
- Car Key/Fob Programming Companies

---

# Why This Market?

Founder experience working in the gate and fencing industry revealed common operational problems:

- Missed calls
- Delayed quotes
- Poor customer communication
- Inefficient route scheduling
- Lost reviews and poor reputation management
- Revenue leakage from poor processes

These businesses are often underserved compared to HVAC, roofing, and plumbing.

---

# Core Offer

Revenue Recovery Assessment™

Deliver a personalized business assessment that:

1. Identifies revenue leaks.
2. Calculates a Revenue Recovery Score™.
3. Estimates annual lost revenue.
4. Recommends automation solutions.
5. Leads to a consultation and implementation offer.
