A personalized business assessment that:

- Recommends automation solutions
- Generates a professional PDF report
- Leads to consulting and implementation services

# Signature Framework

## Competitive Revenue Gap Analysis™

Compare prospects against their highest-performing competitors.

Metrics:

- Google rating
- Review count
- Review velocity
- Customer sentiment
- Communication quality
- Response time
- Automation maturity
- Online presence
- Service availability

Outputs:

- Competitor scorecard
- Revenue opportunity estimate
- Revenue Gap™ estimate
- Competitor review highlights
- Priority action plan

Purpose:

Demonstrate why competitors are winning and how the prospect can catch up.
