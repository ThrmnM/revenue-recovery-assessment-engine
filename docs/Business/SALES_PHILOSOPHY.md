# Sales Philosophy

Version 1.0

Status: Living Document

---

## Mutual Qualification

Not every prospect should become a client.

Both parties should determine whether a long-term working relationship is appropriate.

High-pressure selling has no place in our business.

---

## Honest Recommendations

If a prospect only needs a small solution, we recommend a small solution.

If no solution is required, we say so.

Our objective is to become trusted advisors rather than salespeople.

---

## Contrarian Selling

Sometimes the best way to build trust is to recommend less rather than more.

Long-term relationships consistently outperform large one-time sales.

---

## Education Before Persuasion

The Assessment Report exists to educate.

The sales conversation exists to answer questions.

Clients should arrive at their own conclusions based on evidence.

---

## No Fear-Based Selling

We never exaggerate problems.

We never manufacture urgency.

Evidence speaks for itself.

---

## Referral Mindset

Every interaction should leave the prospect feeling respected.

Even prospects who never become clients should feel comfortable referring others.
