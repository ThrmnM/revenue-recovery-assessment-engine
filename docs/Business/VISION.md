# Vision

Version 1.0

---

The Business Intelligence & Growth Platform exists to become the operating system for field service businesses.

The platform begins by helping businesses understand where they are losing opportunities.

Over time it evolves into a complete operational platform that assists with:

Business Intelligence

Assessments

CRM

Communication

Automation

Reporting

Analytics

Client Portals

Artificial Intelligence

The long-term objective is to create a sustainable platform that improves thousands of small and medium-sized service businesses while creating a legacy that can continue for future generations.
