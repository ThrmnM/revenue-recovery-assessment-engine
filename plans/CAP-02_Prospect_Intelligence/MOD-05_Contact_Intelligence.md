# MOD-05 — Contact Intelligence

Status: Planning

Version: 1.0

Owner: Revenue Recovery System

## Purpose

Contact Intelligence evaluates the health and reliability of every public communication channel.

The objective is to identify broken communication paths before outreach begins and to uncover hidden revenue leakage.
