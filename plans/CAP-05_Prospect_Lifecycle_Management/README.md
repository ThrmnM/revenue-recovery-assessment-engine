# CAP-05 — Prospect Lifecycle Management

## Purpose

Prospect Lifecycle Management manages the complete history of every prospect from first discovery through becoming a long-term client.

Unlike the Business Intelligence Engine, which evaluates the current health of a business, Prospect Lifecycle Management records everything that happens over time.

The objective is to build a permanent business record that can support:

- Multiple outreach campaigns
- Historical assessments
- Timeline tracking
- Service delivery
- Client management
- Future AI insights

This capability ensures no information about a prospect is ever lost.

Every interaction becomes part of the Prospect Master Record.
