# Feature

Business Health Assessment Engine

---

## Objective

Create the central scoring engine for the Revenue Recovery System.

The engine evaluates the digital health of a local service business and produces structured data used by:

- Executive Summary
- Competitive Analysis
- Recommendations
- Revenue Recovery Report
- Prospect Ranking Engine
- Weekly Outreach Queue

---

## Inputs

### Google

- Rating
- Review Count

### Competitor Analysis

- Competitor Review Gap
- Rating Gap

### Revenue

- Estimated Revenue Leakage

### Website

- Exists
- HTTPS
- Mobile Friendly
- Contact Form

### Facebook

- Business Page
- Last Post Date
- Active within 90 Days

### Automation

- Automation Score

---

## Outputs

Overall Business Health Score

Business Grade

Priority Score

Category Scores

Strengths

Weaknesses

Service Opportunities

---

## Service Opportunities

Examples

Poor Reviews

→ Reputation Management

No Website

→ Website Design

Outdated Website

→ Website Refresh

Inactive Facebook

→ Monthly Social Media Management

Low Automation

→ Revenue Recovery Installation
