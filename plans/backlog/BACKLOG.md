### Business Intelligence Engine

Every assessment category should include an **Evidence** section that supports the assigned score with objective data.

Example:

### Google Reputation

**Score**

61 / 100

**Explanation**

Your Google rating is competitive, but your review count is significantly lower than the leading local competitor, reducing your online credibility and social proof.

**Evidence**

Google Rating: 4.3 ★

Review Count: 52

Market Leader Rating: 4.8 ★

Market Leader Reviews: 327

Review Gap: 275

Data Source:
Google Business Profile
